2.Keith's Election1

Keith’s Aunt Deb is on the town council in High Falls. The people ih High Falls have voted for Aunt Deb to represent them three times. Every two years,Keith puts up posters and passes out brochures as part of Aunt Deb’s long campaign for the spot on the council. He has always listened when Aunt Deb argued about the issues in her debates with the other candidates.

Keth told Aunt Deb that his class at High Falls Elementary was going to have an election for the school government this month.Aunt Deb was very excited. She thought Keith should run for president just as she did when she was in school.Keith was not so sure.The elections at his school were not usually very exciting. Most of the time,the most interesting issue being discussed was whether the cafeteria should have more pizza days.He also wasn’t so sure he had a chance to win.Even if he did,he was not sure what he would do once he was class president.

Aunt Deb thought this was really important.Aunt Deb believed that every on shold show an interest in politics in general.She pointed out that since Keith had always helped with her campaigns, she could now help him too. She could help with posters and give him tips on his speech.Keith decided that this was a good idea.He told Mr.Pratt,the student council advisor,that he wanted to be a candidate for class president.
