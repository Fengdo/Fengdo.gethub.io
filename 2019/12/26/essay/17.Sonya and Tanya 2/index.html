17. Sonya and Tanya 2

Over the next couple of days, the girls spent almost all of their time in their rooms with their seeds. They were very friendly girls to begin with. Now it seemed that they had made a pair of new friends, and they did many activities together with their new friends – the seeds. Sonya listened to the radio with her seed, and Tanya read her book out loud to her seed.

在接下来的数天里，她们花费了几乎所有的时间在房间里陪伴种子。她们首先都表现的很友好。现在，看起来像是有了一对新朋友。并且和“新朋友们”种子一起活动。Soya和种子一起听音乐。Tanya大声读书给种子听。

Sonya wanted her seed to grow faster. After two days, she saw only the very top of a sprout coming through her soil. She found her mother to complain.

Sonya想让她的种子快快成长。两天后，她仅仅看到泥土上冒出了一缕新芽。她找了她妈妈然后抱怨。

“Why won’t my seed grow faster?” she asked.

我的种子怎么不快点成长呢？ 她问。

“There you go being ridiculous again! You have to be patient if you want to be a good gardener,” her mother said. “it will take some time before your plant grows tall.”

别再这么荒唐了！如果你想让它们快点长大就得耐心点。 她妈妈说。 要花时间才能长高的。

Sonya was not a very patient girl. She didn’t like waiting for anything, so Sonya groaned at her mother and ran back up to her room. There, she turned the music up louder and turned the lights on even though it was daytime.

Sonya本来就不是一个耐心的人。她不喜欢等待任何东西。Sonya对她妈妈哼了一声就跑到楼上的房间里去了。她把音乐调的更大，然后打开了灯，尽管是白天。

A little while later Tanya came downstairs.

一会儿后，Tanya跑到楼下。

“Mom, what can I do to make my seed grow faster than Sonya’s?” asked Tanya.

“妈，我要怎么做让我的种子长的比Sonya的快？” Tanya 问。

“Oh, you are just like your sister! I’m not going to help you two on this one,” laughed her mother.

“你和你姐妹一样！我不打算在这件事情上帮助你们。” 她妈妈笑了。

Tanya groaned and rant upstairs like her sister. She was also not a patient girl. She read her journal to her plant again, but she read it louder. She asked her plant questions even though she knew there would be no response.

Tanya哼了一声，和她妹妹一样跑到了楼上。她也不是一个耐心的人。她再一次把日记读给植物听，但是她读的更大声了。她问植物一些问题，尽管她知道它们不会回答。

A couple of days later, the house awoke to a scream. Sonya was shouting in her room when the whole family came in. She was very upset.

数天后，房子里发出了喊声。当所有人进来时，Sonya在房间里大叫。她当时非常的生气。

“What’s wrong, Sonya?” asked her mother.

“怎么了？” 她妈妈问道。

“Where’s my seed？！” exclaimed Sonya.

“我的种子不见了！” Sonya 惊呼到。

There，on her window sill，was an empty cup and some dirt. There was no seed，however.

在她的阳台上有一个空的杯子和一些士。无论如何也没看到种子。

“Did you take my seed?!” Sonya asked her sister.

“你拿了我的种子了吗？” Sonya问她的姐妹。

“No，I didn’t take your seed，” shouted Tanya back.

“我没拿” Tanya大声回复。

Hold on girs，slow down. Look here on your floor，I think there’s some evidence in this case.” said their mother.

“稍等，慢点。看地板,那应该是一些证据。”她们的妈妈说。

indeed, the evidence was strong. There was a little set of dirty paw prints leading away from the window sill. It looked like their cat Whiskers did it.

确实，证据很充分。窗台上那里有一块小的脏的爪印。看起来是他们的猫Whiskers做的。

“Oh no, Whiskers ate my plant!” said Sonya and started crying.

“不，Whiskers吃了我的植物！” Sonya说着就开始哭了起来。

Tanya felt very bad for her sister. She didn’t know what to do, but she didn’t care about growing a taller seed than her sister any more.

Tanya为她的姐妹感到同情。她不知道怎么办，但是她不再操心植物了。

“Don’t cry, Sonya,” said Tanya，”we’ll just explain what happened.

“Sonya,别哭” Tanya说，”我们只是弄明白事实的真相”。

You can help me take care of my plant，and we’ll turn in one project.”

你可以帮我照顾我的植物，我们提交同一个项目。

“Really?” said Sonya through red eyes. “You would do that for me?”
she asked.

“真的吗？” Sonya通过过红红的眼睛说。”你会为我那些做？” 她问道。

“Of course! You’re my sister!” said Tanya, and they hugged.

“当然！你是我的姐妹!” Tanya说道，然后拥抱在一起。

Now their mother was crying.

现在她们的妈妈也哭起来了。

“It’s so nice to see you on the same team! Usually, you are opposed to doing anything together.I’m so proud you’re working together,” said their mother as she hugged her two twins tightly.

“很高兴能看到你们这么固结！你们经常反对彼此做任何事情。我为你们的合作感到骄傲。” 她妈妈说着说着，就和她的两个女儿一样紧紧的抱在了一起。
