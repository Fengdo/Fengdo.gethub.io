For a long time the giant squid was thought to be a legend that didnâ€™t exist. In stories, the giant squid was a sea monster that attacked ships. Even when giant squid became stuck on beach shores, they were taken for mermen*! But now we know the giant squid is real. No person has seen them in their natural habitat, but witnesses have glimpsed these creatures above water and sometimes washed up on the shore. Recently, one was even captured and brought onto land.

Just how gigantic is a giant squid? It is one of the biggest animals on earth. The biggest of them Can be almost 60 feet long and weigh over two tons. An adult giant squid is about the size ofa school bus, but a baby can be as small as a cricket, The giant squid has ten limbs. Eight of these are called arms. Two of the limbs are elongated and are called tentacles. The arms and tentacles are covered with sharp suckers that help the squids catch food.

Scientists are stil not sure what these squid eat or how fast they can swim. Like most squid.they probably live no longer than five years, but even this is just a guess. Scientists stil have much to learn about these sea animals. Much of what they know comes from studying the remains of giant squid found in the stomachs of the sperm whales that eat them!
